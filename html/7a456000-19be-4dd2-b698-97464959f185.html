As you probably know, we ran a competition last month for new writers to help launch our paid submissions. We have decided to slightly change our list payment system to make it better for more people.

Now, instead of just getting $100 for your list, the four best lists of each month will win an extra bonus payment of $500! If your list is one of the four we will send an extra $500 to your paypal address at the start of the following month. You will still receive your $100 payment as soon as we approve your list for publication.

For more details (including details on how to send your list in) check out our Write for Listverse page.
