In contrast, synthetic marijuana is a potpourri-like variety of random, dried-out plants, such as herbs. These plants are saturated with chemicals called cannabinoids, which mimic the mind-altering effects of THC, the active ingredient in marijuana.

However, synthetic marijuana is not a natural substance but instead a mixture of various man-made chemicals sprayed on dead plants. In addition, synthetic marijuana is significantly more potent than marijuana, and the level of potency is not regulated or standardized.

3 Drug Tests Can Detect It
