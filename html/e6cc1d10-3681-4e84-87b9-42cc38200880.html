As of today, in celebration of our birthday, we are launching a cookbook concept. What we want is for you to send in your favorite and best recipes and we will put them altogether in to a book. We will probably distribute the book in electronic and hard copy – so you will be able to give them to friends as gifts or keep them on your own bookshelf.

When you send in the recipe, please be sure to use imperial (US) measurements (this makes it easier for us to format consistently). Also, tell us how you want your name to appear in the book – for example we can print your real name and your listverse username, or either of the two. PLEASE also ensure that your recipe is sent in accurately as I may not be able to test all myself.

You can send recipes in of any type – we will categorize the book when all submissions are in. There is no deadline for submissions – once we have enough we will let people know here.
